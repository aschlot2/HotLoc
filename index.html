About HotLoc

HotLoc is a modular heated locker system that helps restaurants store takeout orders securely while warming the order until pickup. Each unit includes controlled heating, PIN access, and a simple interface for staff and customers. Learn more about the team behind HotLoc by clicking here.

HotLoc's Creators

HotLoc was created by Alex Schlotterer, Gabe Jaghnoun, Luke Merrick, Riley Schweer, and Taliesin Carter as their capstone project at the University of Toledo.
